About(Lo)

My name is Lauren but I go by Lo. I am a multimedia journalist and a full stack web developer. After a few years as a radio journalist (NPR, KQED), I decided to switch gears and learn back and front-end web development. I guess I am also know as a 'javascript journalist' and I am striving to combine code and storytelling. I want to create beautiful and interactive multimedia projects in order to enhance the user experience, making news and in-depth reporting complex but accessible. What am I looking for? Well, like everyone else, I thrive on learning new skills and perfecting old ones but right now, I'd like to pursue my web development career until I can fully combine journalism and programming. I am looking for an internship or entry-level position in both front-end and back-end web development, preferably with a company or organization that focuses on storytelling or on producing journalistic content


My journalistic background means that I can also bring powerful storytelling and multimedia skills to any organizations seeking to reach the public in meaningful ways.


On a side note, I also love zombies, technology, design, radio and storytelling.
